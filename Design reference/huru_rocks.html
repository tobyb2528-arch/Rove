Specifications

A roomy backpack for work, everyday errands, or short trips. It fits tech, chargers, several books, and an extra change of clothes. Two separate compartments keep things organised, one of them designed specifically for electronics. Three-layer foam in the shoulder straps lightens the load, while hidden pockets keep documents and valuables safe. A luggage-pass-through strap on the back lets you attach the pack securely to a suitcase.

• Two main compartments: dedicated tech + spacious cargo
• Three‑pocket organiser panel in the main compartment
• Triple‑layer foam shoulder straps
• Rail system for one‑hand strap adjustment & quick‑release sternum strap
• Hidden rear pocket

• Fidlock magnetic key clip
